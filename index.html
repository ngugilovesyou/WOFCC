Welcome Message

A Word from Our Principal

At Word of Faith Community College, we believe that education is the most powerful instrument for transformation. Our mission is not merely to impart skills, but to cultivate men and women of character, competence, and Christ-centred purpose who will serve Kenya and beyond.

Since our founding, we have equipped hundreds of students across nine dynamic departments — from Automotive Engineering and ICT to Counselling Psychology and Christian Ministry. Every course we offer is designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application, ensuring our graduates are ready on day one.

I invite you to explore our programmes, visit our campus in Kiambu, and join our growing family of skilled, purpose-driven professionals.

The Principal, WOFCC

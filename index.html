A Word from Our Principal

At Word of Faith Community College, we believe education is the most powerful instrument for transformation. Our mission is not merely to impart skills, but to cultivate men and women of character, competence, and Christ-centred purpose who will serve Kenya and beyond.

Since our founding, we have equipped hundreds of students across nine dynamic departments — from Automotive Engineering and ICT to Counselling Psychology and Christian Ministry. Every course bridges academic knowledge with real-world application, ensuring our graduates are ready on day one.

I invite you to explore our programmes, visit our Kiambu campus, and join our growing family of purpose-driven professionals.

Rev. Dr. Samuel Nduati, Principal
